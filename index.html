About me

My name is Steven Chapman and before I began this apprenticeship I was a chef for around 10 years. During the pandemic, I lost my job and the hospitality industry was hit hard. I used this opportunity to retrain and re-skill within the digital marketing world. I completed the Bootcamp and learned a lot along the way this gave me the confidence and necessary skills to impress My Car Import during my work placement and the opportunity to get my apprenticeship with them.

About the Compnay

My Car Import has been importing vehicles to the UK from all over the world for the last 25 years. They offer customers wishing to import a vehicle into the UK an easy alternative to having to take on the process by themselves. They have built our business on a professional door-to-door service that covers every aspect of your vehicle import and stops the potential problems if you do not have our team to help you. They have the extensive and detailed knowledge required to decide to import a vehicle to the UK an easy one. My Car Import has around 10 employees, including key people such as Jamie Fuller (Marketing Manager), Jack Charlesworth (Managing Director).

Services include

  • Importing a variety of vehicles from around the world to the United Kingdom.

  • Getting the vehicles registered in the United Kingdom

  • Mot testing vehicles in our in house MOT garage. Iva testing vehicles to conform with UK standards.

  • Personalised CRM portal for customers to upload all the necessary paperwork and for the team to monitor when the vehicle is arriving, being tested, parts being ordered and collection or delivery..

  • Modifications mainly to USA imports, these include light conversions and wheal adjustments.

  • Servicing vehicles that have been bought second hand or have been off the road for a while.
